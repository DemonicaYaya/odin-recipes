Lasagna

Description

This is a classic Italian lasagna with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy cheese. Perfect for family dinners!

It takes 2 hours to make, but it's absolutely worth it

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar pasta sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. In a large pan, cook ground beef with onion and garlic until browned.
  4. Add pasta sauce to the meat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese. Repeat layers.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until cheese is golden.
  8. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
